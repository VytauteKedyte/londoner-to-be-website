Clapton


Situated in East London next to the marshes forming the green belt of London, and next to the River Lea, Clapton offers many outdoor sports opportunities, including a rowing club and a horse riding centre. Clapton boasts the famous Chatsworth Road market, that is much beloved by foodies, as well as a few pubs that have been strongly resisting the gentrification of the neighbourhood, such as Anchor and Hope, and Royal Sovereign. Clapton is a walk away from Dalston if you are up for a night out, or a family-friendly Stoke Newington with plenty of boutiques and cake shops.
